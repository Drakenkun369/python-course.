Lesson 8: Dictionaries and Sets

Dictionaries in Python are unordered collections of key-value pairs. They allow you to store and retrieve data based on keys, unlike lists which are indexed by numbers.

Sets are unordered collections of unique elements. They do not allow duplicate items.
